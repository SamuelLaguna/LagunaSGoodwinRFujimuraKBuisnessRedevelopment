Manteca Tire & Wheel Outlet
Great Service, Unbeatable Price
Manteca Tire & Wheel Outlet is a leader in offering name brand tires, wheels, auto repair and brake services for customers located in and around the Manteca, California area. Our goal is to focus on customer service. It is the foundation of our business. We employ a well-trained staff specializing in the sale and installation of passenger car, performance and light truck or SUV tires. Custom wheels are also available, as well as special wheels and rims to compliment any tire or customer vehicle.

Thanks to our knowledgeable sales associates, Manteca Tires & Wheel is the best place to buy a replacement set. MICHELIN®, BFGoodrich® and Hankook are our most popular brands, but we also carry tires from a number of other reputable manufacturers including Bridgestone, Continental, Dunlop, Falken, Firestone, Goodyear, Kumho, Nexen, Pirelli, Sumitomo, Toyo and Yokohama.

You can schedule regular or low profile installation online or other services such as computer spin balancing, flat repairs and rotations.
